# Trade Launch Intelligence — Agent Skills Plugin

This repository contains an Agent Skill that provides complete business launch 
intelligence for traders and exporters globally. It converts any product idea 
(or no idea at all) into a professional, 13-document advisory package.

## Available Skills

| Skill | Description | Path |
|-------|-------------|------|
| trade-launch-intelligence | Global trade research, market entry strategy, financial modeling, and multi-document advisory package creation | `skills/trade-launch-intelligence/` |

## Specialist Agent Team

This plugin utilizes a professional multi-agent team to ensure deep expertise across all domains:

- **Market Analyst**: Product discovery, global trends, and sourcing.
- **Finance Advisor**: Financial modeling, ROI, and funding.
- **Compliance Expert**: Customs, permits, and trade law.
- **Business Strategist**: Lead orchestrator and risk analyst.

## Capabilities

- 🔍 **Multi-Agent Engine**: Specialized agents handle research, financials, and legal.
- 📄 **13 Professional Documents**: Research reports, master summaries, and outreach materials.
- 🌍 **Global Coverage**: Localized compliance and funding data for 50+ countries.
- 🏦 **Shared Intelligence Ledger**: Cross-agent data synchronization via `.trade_intel_audit.md`.

## Trigger Phrases

Trigger this skill with any of these phrases:

- "I want to start exporting [product] from [country]"
- "Is there money in [product]?"
- "Help me research a product to trade"
- "What should I export from [country]?"
- "I have $5,000 and want to start a trading business"
- "Give me a business plan for exporting cashew nuts"
- "Write me a pitch email for my shea butter export business"

## Standard

This skill follows the [Agent Skills open standard](https://agentskills.io).
