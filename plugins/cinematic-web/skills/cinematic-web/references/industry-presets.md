# Cinematic Web — Industry Presets

When an industry is identified (from discovery, URL, or PRD), load the matching preset
as a STARTING POINT. Always confirm with the user before applying. These are defaults,
not mandates — the user's brand direction always overrides the preset.

---

## SAAS / TECH PRODUCT

**Brand Defaults**
- Tone: Precise · Confident · Forward-thinking
- Color direction: Dark backgrounds (#0A0F1E–#111827), electric accent (#6366F1 / #4FFFB0 / #38BDF8)
- Typography: Inter or Geist (body), Neue Haas Grotesk or Space Grotesk (display)
- CTA default: "Get Started Free" / "Start Building" / "See It In Action"

**Scene Defaults**
- Camera: Slow push-in through geometric space, depth-of-field layers
- 3D: Three.js — particle system or wireframe mesh that reacts to cursor
- Motion signature: Dashboard panels slide in on scroll like a live system loading
- Hero video: Abstract data visualization or code-like particle flow
- Scroll: Smooth with section-level snap on key feature reveals
- Recommended concept contrast: "Deep Signal" vs "Amber Protocol" vs "Void & Bloom"

**Stack Recommendation:** Next.js + Sanity CMS + Supabase auth + Vercel

## LUXURY / FASHION / BEAUTY

**Brand Defaults**
- Tone: Refined · Sensual · Exclusive
- Color direction: Near-black (#0D0D0D), warm gold (#C9A84C), cream (#F5F0E8), or stark white
- Typography: Playfair Display / Cormorant Garamond (headings), Jost / DM Sans (body)
- CTA default: "Discover" / "Explore the Collection" / "Reserve Yours"

**Scene Defaults**
- Camera: Ultra-slow drift across surface textures, extreme close-up reveals
- 3D: Spline — rotating product or abstract sculptural form with material shaders
- Motion signature: Text fades in letter by letter like perfume diffusing
- Hero video: Textile/material in slow motion, or product emerging from shadow
- Scroll: Narrative scroll — each section tells the next chapter of the brand story
- Recommended concept contrast: "Void & Bloom" vs "The Golden Hour" vs "Soft Architect"

**Stack Recommendation:** Astro + Sanity CMS + Cloudinary + Vercel / Netlify

---

## HEALTH / WELLNESS / FITNESS

**Brand Defaults**
- Tone: Energetic · Authentic · Empowering (or: Calm · Healing · Grounded)
- Color direction: Vibrant greens (#22C55E), warm earth (#D97706), white space — OR soft sage/clay/cream
- Typography: DM Sans or Plus Jakarta Sans (body), Syne or Clash Display (headings)
- CTA default: "Start Your Journey" / "Join Today" / "Try Free for 14 Days"

**Scene Defaults**
- Camera: Handheld energy for fitness, or slow nature reveal for wellness
- 3D: None or Rive for animated brand icon/mascot
- Motion signature: Elements bloom and expand on scroll like breathing
- Hero video: Nature landscape (wellness) or athletic motion in slow-mo (fitness)
- Scroll: Smooth parallax, generous whitespace between sections
- Recommended concept contrast: "Wild Precision" vs "The Golden Hour" vs "Soft Architect"

**Stack Recommendation:** Next.js + Contentful + Vercel

---

## FINTECH / FINANCIAL SERVICES

**Brand Defaults**
- Tone: Trustworthy · Intelligent · Efficient
- Color direction: Deep navy (#0B1F4F), white, with a single electric accent (#2563EB / #10B981)
- Typography: Inter (body), Neue Haas Grotesk or IBM Plex Sans (headings)
- CTA default: "Open an Account" / "See How It Works" / "Get Your Free Analysis"

**Scene Defaults**
- Camera: Precise, controlled — slow rise or lateral drift over clean geometric surfaces
- 3D: Three.js — subtle globe or network graph animation (trust-building, not gimmicky)
- Motion signature: Numbers count up on scroll, charts draw themselves
- Hero video: Abstract flowing light lines suggesting money/data movement
- Scroll: Clean section transitions, no snap — dignified, measured pace
- Recommended concept contrast: "The Quiet Authority" vs "Deep Signal" vs "Amber Protocol"

**Stack Recommendation:** Next.js + Supabase + Vercel (compliance-friendly SSR)

---

## REAL ESTATE / PROPERTY

**Brand Defaults**
- Tone: Aspirational · Sophisticated · Place-driven
- Color direction: Stone / slate / charcoal with warm accent (terracotta, gold, sage)
- Typography: Cormorant Garamond / Libre Baskerville (headings), Jost (body)
- CTA default: "View Properties" / "Book a Tour" / "Find Your Home"

**Scene Defaults**
- Camera: Aerial drone-style pullback or slow interior glide (architectural walk-through feel)
- 3D: Spline — 3D building or floor plan that rotates on hover
- Motion signature: Reveals from left like curtains drawing back
- Hero video: Golden-hour aerial over a property, or interior with natural light shift
- Scroll: Parallax on property imagery, section transitions with architectural structure
- Recommended concept contrast: "The Golden Hour" vs "The Quiet Authority" vs "Soft Architect"

**Stack Recommendation:** Next.js + Sanity CMS + Cloudinary + Vercel

---

## CREATIVE AGENCY / STUDIO / PORTFOLIO

**Brand Defaults**
- Tone: Fearless · Unexpected · Master-of-craft
- Color direction: Bold and brand-specific — could be anything. Often: black + one electric color
- Typography: Clash Display / Space Grotesk / Syne (headings), anything unexpected but readable
- CTA default: "See Our Work" / "Let's Build Something" / "Get in Touch"

**Scene Defaults**
- Camera: Whip pan energy, or hyper-slow macro of work-in-process
- 3D: Three.js or Rive — interactive and playful, breaks the grid
- Motion signature: Cursor leaves a trail, or hover on work items triggers video preview
- Hero video: Time-lapse of creative process, or abstract art piece in motion
- Scroll: Horizontal scroll for portfolio grid, vertical for case studies
- Recommended concept contrast: "Void & Bloom" vs "Wild Precision" vs "Mercury Line"

**Stack Recommendation:** Astro or SvelteKit + static + Cloudinary + Netlify/Vercel

---

## ECOMMERCE / PRODUCT

**Brand Defaults**
- Tone: Desirable · Clear · Conversion-optimized (with visual soul)
- Color direction: Follows brand identity closely — product photography is the hero
- Typography: Clean, fast-loading — Inter, DM Sans, or brand-specific
- CTA default: "Shop Now" / "Add to Cart" / "Find Yours"

**Scene Defaults**
- Camera: Product close-up from multiple angles, slow orbit
- 3D: Spline — product viewer that spins on scroll or hover
- Motion signature: Product images snap into place on scroll like a lookbook
- Hero video: Product in use in aspirational lifestyle environment
- Scroll: Lookbook-style horizontal + vertical hybrid
- Recommended concept contrast: Brand-specific — ask for brand adjectives before generating

**Stack Recommendation:** Next.js + Shopify Headless or Medusa.js + Cloudinary + Vercel

---

## UNKNOWN / GENERAL / STARTUP

When the industry is unclear or genuinely multi-category:
- Run the full discovery interview (Phase 0)
- Do NOT apply a preset — generate concepts fresh from discovery answers
- Pay extra attention to Q4 (emotion) and Q5 (inspiration sites) from the interview
- Use the 3-concept contrast framework with no preset bias
